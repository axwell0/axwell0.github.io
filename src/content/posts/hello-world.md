This is a placeholder post. Replace it with your first real article.

Posts are tagged with the content types defined in
`src/config/contentTypes.ts` — for example `article`, `note`, `project`, or
`tutorial`. The tag you put in a post's frontmatter shows up on the Tags page
and groups your content by type.

## Writing a post

Drop a `.md` or `.mdx` file into `src/content/posts/`, give it frontmatter
(`title`, `pubDatetime`, `description`, `tags`), and it appears automatically.
